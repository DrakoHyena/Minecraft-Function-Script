## Removing an MCFS pack from your world
To remove an MCFS pack from your world simiply run the following in the following:
/function mcfs-remove-<packname>-<gibberish>
Where pack name is the pack you're removing and gibberish is just some letters or number that follow the pack name, you should see it in the auto complete.
This will stop the pack from running and remove all scoreboards associated with the pack making clean up quick and easy!
